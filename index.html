EUROVISION SONG CONTEST

The Eurovision Song Contest is an internationally televised songwriting competition, organised by the European Broadcasting Union and featuring participants chosen by EBU member broadcasters representing their countries from across Europe and beyond. Here shows the participating countries including the Big 5, only 26 countries go through to the final each year, this is resulting countries from 2022.

Jury vs Televotes

Each year, every country will give two sets of points (1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 10 and 12) to their favourite songs; one set is given by a jury of five music industry professionals from that country, and one set given by viewers watching the show in country. Viewers can vote by telephone, SMS and through the official app. This is distinguished through the Jury and Televoters
